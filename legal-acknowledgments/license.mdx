## Open Source License

Custom Fields is **100% free** for projects that are:
- Open source (AGPL-3.0 compatible)
- Non-profit organizations
- Educational institutions
- Personal learning projects

<Warning>
AGPL-3.0 requires your entire application to be open source. For private code, you need a commercial license.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is annual revenue calculated?">
    Include all business revenue from the past 12 months plus any funding (seed, VC, grants). For companies less than 12 months old, use projected annual revenue.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Who counts toward the team size limit?">
    Anyone who works with the Custom Fields codebase: employees, contractors, freelancers, and technical consultants. Non-technical staff and end-users don't count.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when we exceed our tier limits?">
    You have 30 days to upgrade after exceeding revenue or team size limits. We understand growth happens! Just upgrade when you cross the threshold.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can agencies use one license for multiple clients?">
    Yes! Your license covers unlimited client projects. The team size limit only applies to your internal team, not your clients.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when the license expires?">
    Your installations continue working forever. You only lose access to updates and support. Renew anytime to regain access.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is the license enforced?">
    Trust-based system with simple license keys. No tracking, no phone-home, just annual self-certification.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between tiers?">
    Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. We'll prorate the difference for upgrades.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
